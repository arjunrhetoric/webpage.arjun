Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET)

Thakur College of Engineering and Technology, located in Kandivali (Mumbai), is a premier institution committed to providing quality technical education. The college focuses on innovation, research, and holistic student development.

Departments

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering Department emphasizes programming, software development, AI, and modern technologies.

Information Technology

The IT Department provides knowledge in data management, networking, web technologies, and cybersecurity.

Electronics & Telecommunication

This department focuses on communication systems, electronics design, and VLSI technologies.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Department specializes in design, thermal engineering, manufacturing, and robotics.
